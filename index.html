Our Philosophy

A sanctuary for
humans and hounds.

Paws Café was born from a simple belief: life is just better when animals are around. We wanted to create a space that didn't force you to choose between a premium artisanal coffee experience and spending time with your best friend.

Here, natural oak meets soft linen. The aroma of freshly ground espresso mingles with the gentle thud of a wagging tail. Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or simply read a book by the window, you'll find a community that feels like home.

Pet Etiquette

Bring your best friend.

We provide everything your pet needs to feel as comfortable as you do. Fresh spring water bowls, complimentary welcome treats, and cozy shaded spots.

  • Well-behaved, leashed pets are welcome indoors and outdoors.
  • Complimentary fresh water stations available throughout.
  • Please ensure your pets are comfortable around others.
  • Ask our staff for our special pet menu items!
